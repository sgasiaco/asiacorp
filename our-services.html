OUR SERVICES

Everything you need in one place

We provide a wide range of professional services to meet your corporate service needs. Our seek to provide every service with a smile, and to your highest level of satisfaction.

Company Incorporation

Preparation of statutory documents and registers. Includes name reservation and company registration filing with e-notification, within one day.

Company Secretary

As your named company secretary we provide full regulatory compliance, maintenance of statutory registers, AGM and filing of Annual Returns.

Registered Office Address

We provide a registered office address for your company. Our mail support service will inform you on any official mail received for your company.

Nominee Director

All Singapore companies must have at least one resident director. We will appoint our nominee director to fulfil the statutory need.

Work Passes

If you wish to relocate to Singapore, we provide work and dependent visa applications for business owners and employees.

Corporate Affairs

We provide full support for any changes to the shareholding or capital structure of your company including document preparation and filing with authorities.

Annual Accounting & Tax

We provide annual accounts and corporate tax preparation as required for filing of Annual Returns with ACRA and IRAS.

Bookkeeping & Payroll

We provide monthly bookkeeping and payroll services to ensure that your company's financials are current and up to date.

GST Registration & Filing

We provide GST Registration where required. Also the preparation and submission of quarterly GST returns with IRAS.

ACRA Annual Returns

Singapore companies must hold Annual General Meeting (AGM) and duly file Annual Returns with ACRA. We manage the documentation and filing.

Licenses & Permits

Some classes of business activities will require appropriate licenses and permits. We will provide advise and necessary applications for this.

Trademark Application

Trademark registrations provide intellectual property rights protection for a business's identity, symbol or brand. We will help with application.
